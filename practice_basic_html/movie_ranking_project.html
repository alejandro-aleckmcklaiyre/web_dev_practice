The Best Movie (so far...)

My top 3 movies.

The Matrix (1999) | Sci-Fi, Action

Directed by The Wachowskis

The action scenes are super cool, especially the slow-motion bullet-dodging moments. But it’s not just about fighting; the movie also makes you think about reality and free will.

Why watch? If you like action, cool effects, and movies that make you question everything, this one’s for you.

Enola Holmes (2020) | Mystery, Adventure

Directed by Harry Bradbeer

The story follows her as she tries to solve a mystery while also dealing with people who think girls shouldn’t be detectives. Millie Bobby Brown does an amazing job, and the way she talks directly to the camera makes it feel like you’re part of the adventure.

Why watch? It’s a fun, lighthearted mystery with a great lead character and some good laughs along the way.

Spirited Away (2001) | Fantasy, Adventure

Directed by Hayao Miyazaki

This is one of the best animated movies ever! It’s about a girl named Chihiro who accidentally enters a magical world filled with spirits, strange creatures, and a creepy bathhouse run by a powerful witch. She has to work hard, be brave, and find a way to save her parents, who got turned into pigs (yep, that happens). The animation is stunning, and the story feels like a dream—mysterious, beautiful, and sometimes a little scary.

Why watch? If you love unique animation, fantasy worlds, and a story that feels magical yet meaningful, this is a must-watch.
